My name is Xifeng Gao. I come from Shandong province in China. I am a fifth year Ph.D. student in Computer Science department, University of Houston. I belong to two research groups, which are lead by Dr. Zhigang Deng and Dr. Guoning Chen, respectively. I received both my M.S. (advised by Prof. Caiming Zhang) and B.S. degrees from the college of Computer Science at Shandong University.

My research interests are Computer Graphics, Image Processing, and Multimedia Security. Currently I am focusing on topics in geometric modelling and geometry processing. I have worked on various topics, e.g., hex-meshing and optimization, MRI data registration, and 3D model watermarking.
