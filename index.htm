My name is Xifeng Gao. I come from Shandong province in China. I am working with Daniele Panozzo as a PostDoc since Aug. 2016 at the Courant Institute of Mathematical Sciences in New York University. I obtained my Ph.D. in Computer Science from University of Houston in May. 2016. My Ph.D. advisors were Zhigang Deng and Guoning Chen, respectively. I received both my M.S. (advised by Caiming Zhang) and B.S. degrees in the school of Computer Science at Shandong University.

My research interests are Computer Graphics, Image Processing, and Multimedia Security. At the moment, I am focusing on topics in generating and processing models. previously, I worked on various topics, e.g., hex-meshing and optimization, MRI data registration, and 3D model watermarking.
