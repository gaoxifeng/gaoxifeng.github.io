Xifeng Gao


Assistant Professor @ Florida State University | gao@cs.fsu.edu | CV
I have wide research interests in fields related to Geometry Computing, such as Computer Graphics, Visualization, Multimedia Processing, Medical Imaging, and Digital Fabrication.

My current focus is on improving the robustness of geometry processing methods, providing reliable solutions for applications in Computer Graphics, Computer-aided Engineering (CAE) analysis, Computational Fabrication, and Robotics.



I have openings for (under)graduates. If interested, please contact me with your CV!
