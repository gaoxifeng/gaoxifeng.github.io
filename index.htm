Xifeng Gao


Principal Researcher @ Lightspeed Studios at Tencent America | xifgao@tencent.com
I have wide research interests in fields related to Geometry Computing, such as Computer Graphics, Fabrication, and Robotics.

My current focus is on designing gaming oriented geometry processing algorithms, using techniques in computational geometry, rendering, machine learning, and numerical analysis.
We have multiple R&D intern openings for working on geometry processing problems, such as re-meshing, deformation, UV optimization, texture baking, etc. If interested, please let me know.
