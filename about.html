My name is Xifeng Gao. I come from Shandong province of China. Since August, 2016, I have been working with Daniele Panozzo as a PostDoc at the Courant Institute of Mathematical Sciences of New York University. I obtained my Ph.D. degree in Computer Science from University of Houston in May, 2016. My Ph.D. advisors were Zhigang Deng and Guoning Chen. I received both my M.S. (advised by Caiming Zhang) and B.S. degrees in the school of Computer Science of Shandong University.

My research interests are Computer Graphics, Image Processing, and Multimedia Security. At the moment, I am focusing on topics in geometric modeling and geometry processing. Previously, I worked on various topics, e.g., hex-meshing and optimization, MRI data registration, and 3D model watermarking.
